About Us

Neomandi has been started with an objective to provide the Indian farmer with the state of the art technological ecosystem to market their produce by creating a large pool of buyers. Apart from this, necessary infrastructure for scientific preservation, logistical support and pre-market value addition services will be provided to the farmers.

Neomandi's e-platform which brings the coveted transparency and efficiency to the markets by bringing markets to the doorsteps of farmers with its world class infrastructure and internet technology which empowers the farmers to achieve a fair price for the produce and enables the traders to face the debilitating challenges of markets consequently bringing a smile on the end consumer's face, creating a happy nation.
